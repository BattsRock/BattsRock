About the company

BattsRock Group is designed as a flexible umbrella company for creating, managing, and scaling brands. Instead of operating as a single-purpose business, the platform supports multiple ventures with a shared foundation in strategy, operations, and commercialization.

🏛️

Umbrella structure

A parent platform that houses multiple brands, initiatives, and launch projects under one strategic direction.

🚀

Zentra as launch arm

Zentra focuses on market entry, audience validation, messaging, offers, and launch execution for new ventures.

⚙️

Operational backbone

Shared systems, workflows, and support functions reduce friction and make each new brand easier to launch and run.

The launch platform

Zentra sits inside the parent company as the go-to-market function. It is the part of the business built to move ideas from concept to traction with a repeatable process.

01

Positioning & offer design

Clarify the market, define the brand angle, refine the value proposition, and shape a compelling first offer.

02

Launch & acquisition

Build the website, messaging, outreach, and customer entry points needed to validate and acquire early demand.

03

Operations & enablement

Set up the workflows, support structure, and operating rhythm needed for a brand to move beyond launch mode.

04

Scale & portfolio support

Once traction exists, the parent company supports optimization, growth decisions, and the expansion of the portfolio.

How brands fit in
